About Me




Audra has had a passion for hair and a deep interest in the beauty industry since a young age. As an artist at heart, growing up in New Yok, she found a way to apply her passion. She endeavors to create a personal image to each and every client. Her clientele is extremely diverse allowing her to create and master a vast range of styles. Audra strives to stay at the forefront of her field. She has been licensed for 5 years and she continues to take classes to keep up to date with new trends, products and techniques. With her experience, she has mastered a wide range of hair techniques including long hair, cutting, up-do's and also men’s styling. She has specialized in cutting-edge coloring techniques, foil highlights and lowlights, color correction, gray reduction, fashion cuts, modern cuts, razor cuts, men’s cuts and styling. She stays updated on all the latest trends so that she can give each client the look she or he wants. Her specialty and passion is color. Audra is an expert in using a wide range of Alfaparf and Wella color products.
